About Coastal Soul

Coastal Soul's founder and love ambassador, LaNicia Williams' soul food has made a name for itself as a rare gem on the North Oregon Coast. According to LaNicia, the most important ingredient in her soul food is love, and you can taste it in every bite.

LaNicia shared her collard greens recipe with Upper Left Edge online, but her fried chicken recipe is, and forever will remain, a secret!
