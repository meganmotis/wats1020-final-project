Services

Coastal Soul offers a range of soul food services to the North Oregon Coast:

  • Private cooking classes
  • Personal chef/In-home dining
  • Catering for any occasion

Menu

Coastal Soul's specialies include:

  • Fried chicken or lobster (with gluten-free option)
  • Shrimp and grits
  • Collard greens (with vegetarian options)
  • Cornbread
  • Macaroni and cheese
  • Dirty rice
  • Black eye peas
  • Red beans and rice
  • Candied yams
